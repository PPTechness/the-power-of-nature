Safety Guidelines

  • No personal information: Students should never share real names, addresses, or personal details
  • School equipment only: All activities use school devices and accounts
  • Teacher supervision: All online activities require teacher oversight
  • Safe content: Pre-approved websites and educational platforms only
  • Report concerns: Clear process for students to report uncomfortable situations
ğŸ“¸

Image Permissions

  • Photo consent: Check permissions before any camera activities
  • School policy: Follow your school's image sharing guidelines
  • Gallery moderation: All student uploads require teacher approval
  • No faces: Encourage focus on work/objects, not people
  • Copyright respect: Only use own images or copyright-free resources
âš ï¸

Report a Concern

If a student reports feeling uncomfortable or sees inappropriate content:

  1. Take the concern seriously and thank them for reporting
  2. Do not investigate yourself - escalate immediately
  3. Contact your designated safeguarding lead
  4. Document the incident according to school policy
  5. Provide reassurance and support to the student
